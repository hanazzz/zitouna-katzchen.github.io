How To Make a Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

  1. Get out your ingredients (bread, peanut butter, jelly) and a plate and a knife.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one piece of bread.
  3. Spread jelly on the other piece of bread.
  4. Put the two pieces of bread together (fillings facing each other).
  5. Enjoy!
